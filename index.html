AI-Augmented Workflow Mapper

A practical tool to integrate AI into your daily work by focusing on habits, not just technology.

1

Mindset Check: Before You Begin

It's a Conversation, Not a Command

Think of your AI as a new colleague you're managing, not a search engine you're commanding.

The Goal is Augmentation

You are the expert; the AI is your assistant. Look for ways to enhance your skills and process.

2

Map Your Current Process

Choose a recurring task and list every single step you take from start to finish. Be detailed!

3

Identify AI Augmentation Opportunities

Review the steps you listed above. Where could AI help? Look for points of friction, repetitive tasks, or areas that need a "first draft."

  • Which step is the most repetitive?
  • Where do I need to synthesize lots of information?
  • Where could a first draft accelerate my process?
4

Engage & Validate with T-A-R-C

Use this conversational framework for the best results. Treat the AI's first response as a draft and refine it through dialogue.

T

Task

Give an explicit and clear task.

A

Add Context

Provide necessary background info.

R

Refine

Engage in dialogue to improve the output.

C

Chain

Give it the next logical task in your workflow.

Trust but Verify

Remember, you are the final quality control. Always fact-check and validate the AI's output before using it, just as you would review the work of a human team member.
